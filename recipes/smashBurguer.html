Smasshhh Burguer!

Description

A smash burger is a classic comfort food, boasting a thin, flavorful patty smashed and seared on a hot griddle. Topped with melted cheese and classic fixings, it's a crispy, juicy delight packed between soft buns, perfect for indulging in savory satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (80/20 blend), 4 ounces per patty
  • Salt and Pepper, to taste
  • Cheese Slices, 1 slice per burger
  • Burger Buns, 1 per burger
  • Cooking Oil (such as vegetable oil), for cooking the patties
  • Condiments and Toppings (such as ketchup, mustard, lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles), as desired

Steps

  1. Preheat a griddle or skillet over high heat.
  2. Divide ground beef into small portions and form into balls.
  3. Season each ball with salt and pepper.
  4. Place a ball of beef onto the hot surface and immediately smash it flat with a spatula.
  5. Flip the patty and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add cheese on top of the patty and allow it to melt.
  7. Toast the burger buns on the griddle.
  8. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun and adding desired toppings.
  9. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately. Enjoy your delicious smash burger!

Smasshhh Burguer!

Description

A smash burger is a mouthwatering delight that combines simplicity with irresistible flavor. To create the perfect smash burger, start with high-quality ground beef, preferably an 80/20 blend to ensure a juicy and flavorful patty. Season the beef generously with salt and pepper, allowing the natural flavors to shine through. Heat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over high heat until smoking hot, then divide the beef into equal portions and form them into balls. Place each ball onto the hot surface and immediately smash them flat with a spatula, creating a thin patty that will develop a deliciously crispy crust.

As the patties sizzle away, resist the temptation to move them too soon, allowing the crust to form undisturbed. After a couple of minutes, flip the patties and add slices of your favorite cheese on top to melt and meld with the beef. While the cheese melts, lightly toast the buns until golden brown and slightly crispy. Once the patties are cooked to your desired level of doneness and the cheese is gooey and melted, assemble the burgers by placing each patty on a bun and topping them with your preferred condiments and fixings. Whether it's classic ketchup and mustard, crisp lettuce and tomato, or tangy pickles and onions, the possibilities for customization are endless. Bite into the smoky, juicy perfection of a smash burger, and savor the explosion of flavors with every delicious bite.

Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is an exquisite Italian dessert, known for its layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and creamy mascarpone mixture. With a hint of cocoa powder dusted on top, it's a luscious treat that perfectly balances the flavors of coffee, sweetness, and richness.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers (savoiardi biscuits), about 24
  • Strong Brewed Coffee, 1 cup
  • Mascarpone Cheese, 16 ounces
  • Granulated Sugar, 1/2 cup
  • Heavy Cream, 1 cup
  • Vanilla Extract, 1 teaspoon
  • Cocoa Powder, for dusting

Steps

  1. Brew strong coffee and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. In a mixing bowl, beat mascarpone cheese and sugar until smooth.
  3. In another bowl, whip heavy cream and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  4. Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until well combined.
  5. Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee for a few seconds, ensuring they are soaked but not soggy.
  6. Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish.
  7. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  8. Repeat with another layer of soaked ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  9. Smooth the top and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to set.
  10. Before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder for a finishing touch. Enjoy your heavenly tiramisu!
